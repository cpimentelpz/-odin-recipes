My favorite dish is ñoquis


Description


Ingredients

    1 ¾ cups finely chopped fresh basil
    2 small tomatoes, finely chopped
    2 ⅓ cups fine dry bread crumbs
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
    1 teaspoon sea salt
    ½ cup olive oil

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together the basil, tomatoes, bread crumbs, garlic, jalapeno, sea salt and olive oil.
  2. Cover, and refrigerate overnight. Serve with your favorite dippers!
  3. Serve with your favorite dippers!
